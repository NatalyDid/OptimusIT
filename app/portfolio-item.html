Aviwest – video contribution uplink systems for live streaming.
DONE:

Optimus IT developed the AVIWEST-booking website, the purpose of which is to help the AVIWEST customers to rent or loan AVIWEST systems and get useful information thanks to a community of users.

AVIWEST is a global provider of video hybrid contribution systems for live streaming over bonded 3G/4G cellular and satellite networks. By providing broadcasters with cost-effective, reliable, and mobile video contribution systems designed to withstand harsh environmental and varying network conditions anywhere in the world, AVIWEST ensures broadcasters can effectively respond to the challenges of remote live newsgathering. Headquartered in Saint-Grégoire, France, AVIWEST operates worldwide through its sales offices and distribution network.
